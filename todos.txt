+ user permissions:
    - read-only (get and get all)
    - hardcoded (cannot be altered in any way by anyone except devs)
    - many-to-many relationship with user roles (no inverse field needed)

+ user_roles
    - USER, ADMIN and MODERATOR roles are hardcoded and must be readonly (must not be altered in any way by anyone except devs)
    - user roles are subject to crud operations
    - many-to-many relationship with user roles
    - one-to-many relationship with users
